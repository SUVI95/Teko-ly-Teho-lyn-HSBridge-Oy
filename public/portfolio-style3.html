About

From Latin America to Helsinki — building the bridge.

I grew up between languages and cultures. That shaped how I think about talent: it doesn't live where opportunity does — and the gap between them is a systems problem.

Today I build that bridge. I design AI-assisted recruitment pipelines, automated outreach engines, and content funnels that move skilled candidates from where they are to where they're needed.

I think in systems. I ship fast. I care about the human on the other side of the funnel — because every hire is a life moving across borders.
